# Security Policy (VDP)

## ⚠️ Security Notice
At Sovereign AI, we take security seriously. If you have discovered a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

## Reporting a Vulnerability
Do **NOT** open a public GitHub issue for security vulnerabilities. Instead, please email your findings to:
**[security@sovereign-ai.com](mailto:pranaya.mathur@yahoo.com)**

Please include:
- A descriptive subject line.
- Description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce (POC).
- Potential impact.

## Scope
The scope includes the core Sovereign AI platform, the 3-tier detection engine, and official integrations.

## Safe Harbor
We will not take legal action against you provided you:
1.  Do not perform social engineering or DDoS attacks.
2.  Do not access, modify, or delete user data.
3.  Give us a reasonable amount of time to fix the issue before public disclosure.

## Rewards
While we are a small project, we acknowledge significant contributions in our **hall-of-fame** and offer a professional reference for security researchers who help us secure the platform.
